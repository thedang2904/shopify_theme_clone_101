A stunning transformation

Posted by Jim Rothfork on April 28, 2017

From bare studs, to a beautiful new living space. When Mark and Wendy first purchased their house in San Diego, it hardly resembled a home. Bare studs, mold & mildew abound, the couple had to completely gut and renovate every square inch of the structure. After eight months, the space (particularly the living room) is unrecognizable to the original, and is now the center of their space
